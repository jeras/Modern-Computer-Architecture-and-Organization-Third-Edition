# Chapter 9, Exercise 6

Do i.MX RT1060 processors support floating-point operations in hardware? Explain your answer.

# Answer
Section 2.2, **Features**, in the reference manual lists the following capability: **Single-precision and double-precision FPU (Floating Point Unit)**.

**The i.MX RT1060 processors support floating-point operations in hardware**.
